About me

DJ Marky Boy

I’m DJ Marky Boy – full of energy, big beats, and party vibes!

I have Autism and ADHD, and that’s my superpower. It’s what makes me creative, enthusiastic, and brilliant at getting people excited. I bring that spark to every event – whether it’s a kids’ party, a silent disco, a wedding, or a karaoke night.

I love GBX, hardstyle, and rawstyle – and I’ve got the playlists to prove it. I’m great at organising games, reading the crowd, and making sure everyone feels included and safe.

A couple of quick things:

🚫 No fireworks

🎈 No balloons (unless helium!)

My hours are flexible, I work with a deposit, and I’m all about making your event unforgettable.
